Meet PEARL

Passive Early Alert Recon Listening device

PEARL Unit

A passive acoustic and RF listening device that detects drones before they become a threat — no active emissions, no radar signature. It simply listens, and when a threat is detected, it gives civilians the critical seconds they need.

Passive Detection

Our Story

Born from the front line

Our founder spent time in active conflict zones and witnessed first-hand how the gap between a drone's approach and a civilian's awareness is measured in seconds people don't have.

The technology to close that gap exists — but at £50,000–£200,000 per system, it was never built for the people who need it most. PEARL changes that.

One PEARL unit is currently deployed and operational, validating the technology in one of the world's most active drone threat environments.
