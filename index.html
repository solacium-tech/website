Meet PEARL

Passive Early Alert Recon Listening device

PEARL Unit

A passive acoustic and RF listening device that detects drones before they become a threat — no active emissions, no radar signature. It simply listens, and when a threat is detected, it gives civilians the critical seconds they need.

Passive Detection

No RF emissions. No radar cross-section. Completely covert operation.

Building-Mounted

Mounts to any building exterior. Minimal installation, maximum coverage.

Networked Nodes

Deploy as standalone units or link into a wide-area detection network.

Vehicle Variant

Mobile-mounted variant in development for convoy and field operations.

How It Works

Simple to deploy.
Impossible to miss.

01

Listens

PEARL passively monitors acoustic and RF frequencies 24/7, requiring no active transmission and leaving no detectable signature.

02

Identifies

On-board processing analyses the signal in real time, matching acoustic and RF signatures against known UAV profiles.

03

Alerts

An instant alert is triggered — audible, visual, or networked — giving people the critical seconds needed to take cover or evacuate.
